Mahan Lit Drop:
A Viral Volunteer Tool

This prototype explores how to turn a single lit drop into a self-spreading network of neighborhood volunteers.

What's a lit drop?

The Mahan campaign uses door hanger flyers to spread the word across neighborhoods. Volunteers walk their area and hang them on doorknobs — it's personal, visible, and effective.

~10 houses = 1 vote
Every lit drop counts
By signing up, you'll drive more votes for the campaign and help change California.

This prototype shows how it works

1
Sign up — enter your info to get lit drops sent to you
2
Get set up — download the app, pick your time and starting point
3
Pick a route — choose from the best options near you
4
Walk & deliver — complete the lit drop at your own pace
5
See your impact — leaderboard, social sharing, and invite friends
Context: This is a concept prototype for Matt Mahan's 2026 gubernatorial campaign. The goal is to maximize lit drop coverage across California by making it dead simple for one volunteer to recruit the next.
