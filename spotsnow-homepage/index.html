How it works

From first brief to final report — four steps, one platform.

🎯
Step 1

Target & Budget

Tell us who you're reaching and what you want to spend. We match you with the right shows across demographics, interests, and geography — with transparent CPMs and no hidden fees.

📋
Step 2

Plan & Tune

We generate a campaign plan across shows and episodes. See projected reach, frequency, and timing — then swap shows, adjust your mix, and dial it in until it's perfect.

🚀
Step 3

Launch

Upload your creative, choose your ad style (host-read, pre-produced, or dynamic), and go live. We handle placement and trafficking — you just approve and launch.

📊
Step 4

Track & Optimize

Watch impressions, engagement, and attribution in real time. Shift budget to what's working mid-campaign and download reports when you're done.
