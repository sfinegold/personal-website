Point construction & shot selection
Good progress on building points with the forehand cross-court. The pattern of going cross-court until a short ball, then attacking down the line, is becoming more natural. Need to be more patient in neutral rallies instead of going for too much too early. Backhand slice is effective as a change of pace but use it less on high balls.

January 18, 2026
Serve mechanics & placement
Serve speed is consistent in the mid-90s for first serves. The main area to improve is placement variety — too many serves are going to the same spot (T on deuce, body on ad). Work on the wide serve on both sides. Second serve needs more spin and less flat to reduce double fault rate. Toss slightly more to the left for the slice serve.
Drills: Serve placement targets (20 min), Second serve spin drill (10 min)
Rating: 6/10
January 4, 2026
Net play & transition game
Volley technique is solid when at the net. The issue is getting there — approach shots are often too short or too central, giving the opponent a passing angle. Focus on deeper approach shots down the line and closing to the net faster. Split step before the opponent hits is critical. Good instincts on overhead placement.
Drills: Approach-volley patterns (20 min), Overhead positioning (10 min)
Rating: 6/10
November 9, 2025
Pre-point routine & mental game
Introduced a structured pre-point routine: towel, bounce, breathe, visualize target. The routine helps with consistency between points and reduces rushing. When the routine was followed, first serve percentage went up noticeably. Keep reinforcing this every session. Also discussed managing energy and focus during long three-set matches.
Drills: Routine practice during live points (30 min)
Rating: 7/10
