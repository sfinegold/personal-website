# SwingVision App Complaints

All user complaints collected during product review — February 2026.

---

## Navigation & Information Architecture

- **Home tab feels directionless**: "It's not clear what I'm supposed to do on this."
- **How-To Guides are invisible**: "I never click the How-To Guides. I wouldn't know to go there."
- **Rewards shouldn't be a whole tab**: "It's good to have a place for that, but I don't feel like it should be a whole tab." Referral program occupies prime navigation for something rarely used.
- **Find Players feels uncomfortable**: "The Players tab I don't really feel comfortable reaching out to people on that."

## Content & Metrics

- **Shot volume over quality**: The app emphasizes competing on number of shots hit, but the user cares about whether they're playing well, not how many balls they hit.
- **Compete tab misaligned**: "Deep Shots In" leaderboard measures volume, not quality. Doesn't tell you if someone is a good player — just that they play a lot.
- **Me tab lacks meaningful stats**: "The tab I go to the most is Me because that just has my sessions and details, but it doesn't have any of the stats I care about."
- **Session detail emphasizes wrong things**: Shot counts (245 shots, 253 shots) shown prominently instead of quality metrics like % in, speed, or placement accuracy.

## Social & Community

- **Don't watch other people's sessions**: "I don't really use this" — the social/feed features don't provide value.
- **Would prefer highlight reels**: "It could be more engaging if they were like highlight reels from these sessions that might be interesting and players that are at a similar level to me."
- **Don't care about referring**: "Things I don't care about as much are how to refer people and how to find players."

## Recording & Session Capture

- **Post-session photo tagging is tedious**: Having to manually tag/identify photos after a session is annoying and time-consuming.
- **No ML pre-selection for player identification**: The app should use machine learning to pre-select/identify players automatically and flag issues, rather than requiring manual tagging after every session.

## Scoring & Stats

- **Auto-scoring frequently fails**: Auto-scoring doesn't work a lot of the time, which means stats aren't available for those sessions.
- **Stats locked behind auto-score**: Shot quality stats and analysis aren't shown unless auto-scoring works correctly. The stats should still be available even when scoring has issues.
- **Rigid scoring format**: The app only supports traditional scoring formats (e.g., standard sets). Non-standard scores like ending at 5-5 and playing a tiebreak can't be entered. Example: "Yesterday we ended at 5-5 and then played a tiebreak, but I couldn't score it because it wasn't following the exact format."
- **Stats should be decoupled from scoring**: Even if the score for a session is wrong or can't be recorded in the expected format, the shot quality data (speeds, placement, % in) should still be accessible.

## Website

- **Blank page on open**: Opening the swing.vision website from the app leads to a blank page with no content — broken experience.

---

## Core User Need (Summary)

> "The things I care about as a user are seeing my sessions and understanding how good I am and how to improve."

The app should be restructured around this: **my sessions, how good I am, how to improve.**
